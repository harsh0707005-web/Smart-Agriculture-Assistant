Farming Tips & Best Practices

üå± Seasonal Guide

Best Planting Seasons

  • Kharif (Monsoon): Rice, Cotton, Maize, Soybean
  • Rabi (Winter): Wheat, Mustard, Chickpea, Barley
  • Zaid (Summer): Vegetables, Watermelon, Cucumber
  • Plan crop rotation for better soil health
üíß Water Conservation

Smart Irrigation Tips

  • Use drip irrigation to save 40-60% water
  • Water crops early morning or late evening
  • Mulching reduces water evaporation
  • Harvest rainwater for irrigation needs
